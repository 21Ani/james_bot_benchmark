PRODUCT: SecureVault IAM
VERSION: 6.1
DOCUMENT TYPE: Security and Compliance Policy

OVERVIEW
SecureVault IAM provides identity governance, privileged access management, and audit trails
for regulated industries including healthcare and financial services.

PASSWORD POLICY
Minimum length is 14 characters with at least one uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol.
Password rotation is required every 90 days for privileged accounts.
Password history prevents reuse of the last 12 passwords.

ACCOUNT LOCKOUT
Accounts lock after 5 failed login attempts within 15 minutes.
Locked privileged accounts require Security Operations approval for manual unlock.
Service accounts are exempt from lockout but require certificate-based authentication.

COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORKS
SecureVault supports HIPAA, PCI-DSS 4.0, and GDPR control mappings out of the box.
Audit logs are immutable and retained for 5 years.
Quarterly access reviews are mandatory for all admin roles.

INCIDENT RESPONSE
Severity-1 incidents (active breach) require notification to the CISO within 30 minutes.
Customer notification for data exposure follows regulatory timelines (72 hours for GDPR).
Post-incident reports are delivered within 10 business days.

LICENSING
Per-user licensing applies to workforce identities.
Privileged access seats are licensed separately at 3x standard user cost.
Emergency break-glass accounts are limited to 2 per environment.

VENDOR INTEGRATION
SAML 2.0 and OIDC are supported for IdP federation.
Legacy LDAP sync is available only for customers on extended support (EOS date: 2027-06-30).
API keys for automation expire after 180 days unless rotated.

SUPPORT HOURS
Standard support: Monday-Friday 09:00-18:00 customer local time.
Premium support: 24x7 for Severity-1 and Severity-2 incidents.
Onsite support is available for Enterprise customers with NDA in place.
