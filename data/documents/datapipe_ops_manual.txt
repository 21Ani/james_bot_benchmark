PRODUCT: DataPipe Analytics Suite
VERSION: 2.8
DOCUMENT TYPE: Internal Operations Manual

OVERVIEW
DataPipe Analytics Suite ingests batch and streaming data for enterprise BI dashboards.
It integrates with Snowflake, BigQuery, and on-premise SQL Server instances.

DATA RETENTION
Raw event data is retained for 13 months by default.
Aggregated metrics are retained for 7 years for compliance reporting.
Customers may request early deletion under GDPR Article 17 with legal approval.

API RATE LIMITS
Standard tier: 1,000 requests per minute per API key.
Enterprise tier: 10,000 requests per minute with burst allowance of 15,000 for 60 seconds.
Exceeding limits returns HTTP 429 with Retry-After header.

BACKUP AND DISASTER RECOVERY
Full backups run daily at 01:30 UTC; incremental backups every 6 hours.
Recovery Point Objective (RPO) is 6 hours; Recovery Time Objective (RTO) is 4 hours.
Cross-region failover is enabled only for Enterprise Plus contracts.

ONBOARDING TIMELINE
Standard onboarding completes in 10 business days.
Enterprise onboarding with custom connectors requires 25 business days.
Pilot customers receive a dedicated solutions engineer for the first 30 days.

SECURITY COMPLIANCE
DataPipe is SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified.
PII fields must be tagged using the `pii:true` schema annotation.
Penetration test reports are shared with Enterprise customers annually.

BILLING DISPUTES
Billing disputes must be filed within 20 days of invoice date.
Credits for service outages exceeding SLA thresholds are applied automatically within one billing cycle.
Manual credits require VP Finance approval for amounts above $25,000.
