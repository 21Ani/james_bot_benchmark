PRODUCT: CloudSync Pro Enterprise
VERSION: 4.2
DOCUMENT TYPE: Product Knowledge Base

OVERVIEW
CloudSync Pro Enterprise is a secure file synchronization platform for distributed teams.
It supports SSO, audit logging, and role-based access control across 400+ product SKUs.

WARRANTY POLICY
CloudSync Pro Enterprise includes a 36-month hardware warranty and a 24-month software warranty.
Warranty claims must be submitted within 15 business days of incident discovery.
Extended warranty (48 months) is available only for Platinum-tier customers.

SLA AND UPTIME
The Platinum SLA guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime.
Critical incident response time is 1 hour; high-priority incidents within 4 hours.
Scheduled maintenance windows occur every Sunday 02:00-04:00 UTC.

REFUND AND CANCELLATION
Annual subscriptions are refundable within 45 days of purchase if fewer than 500 GB has been synced.
Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable after 7 days.
Enterprise contracts require 90-day written notice for cancellation.

PASSWORD AND ACCESS RECOVERY
Administrators can reset user passwords via the Admin Console > Security > Identity.
MFA recovery codes are issued once during onboarding and can be regenerated by Tier-2 support only.
Locked accounts auto-unlock after 30 minutes unless flagged for suspicious activity.

PRICING
Starter plan: $12/user/month (100 GB storage).
Business plan: $29/user/month (1 TB storage, SSO included).
Enterprise plan: custom pricing with dedicated support pod and 99.95% SLA.

SUPPORT ESCALATION
Tier-1 support handles password resets and billing inquiries.
Tier-2 support handles integration failures and API errors.
Tier-3 engineering is engaged for data corruption or security incidents.
